Discover the importance of irrigation systems in modern agricultural practices. Irrigation plays a critical role in supplying water to crops, especially in regions with limited rainfall. By providing the right amount of water at the right time, farmers can optimize crop growth, improve yield, and mitigate the impact of droughts. Various irrigation methods, such as drip irrigation and sprinkler systems, offer efficient water use and minimize water wastage.
