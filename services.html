Our Services

Data is the new oil. However, Data is only well generated and managed using digital systems.

Modern Agronomy Services

Through advanced soil testing services and agronomy advisory, we empower farmers with modern technologies and best practices, optimizing crop yields and promoting sustainable farming methods.

Comprehensive Digitization

We digitize collection of agricultural initiatives across various value chains like mango, avocado, and coffee through their respective cooperatives. This process ensures efficient data management and empowers farmers with valuable insights.

Global Connectivity

Farmers are connected to international consumers through Murang'a eCommerce/GUUD for cross-border trade and e-commerce opportunities. This digital platform opens new avenues for market expansion and increased profitability.

Local Market Access

The FoodFarmacies network directly connects farmers to local markets, offering fresh and healthy produce. By bridging the gap between farmers and consumers, we're promoting community health and economic growth.

Efficient Data Management

Our digitized data collection pipeline streamlines the process for crop officers, facilitating critical data collection for intergovernmental reporting and policy development, paving the way for informed decision-making.

Personalized Advisory Services

Through collaboration with Partners, we've developed farmer advisory AI tools, providing personalized guidance and support to smallholder farmers, enhancing agricultural productivity and resilience.
