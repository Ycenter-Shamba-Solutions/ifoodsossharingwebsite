Why Choose Us

Accuracy and Trustworthy

Our platform uses the Blockchain technology to ensure data immutability and authenticity, providing peace of mind to all actors involved in the food system. The iFoodSOS API assures its users that the data they use is accurate and trustworthy used and derived from data use cases that in their capture have promoted innovation at the various levels in the contributing organizations and inspired with good open data principles from Data use Working Group levels.

Food Security

In aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goal (UNDG) 2, to end hunger by promoting food security, and UNDG 1, to end to poverty in all its manifestations, we are committed to promoting the use of data to improve food security, create a sustainable food system, in the South-South. This agenda also helps realize the agenda of NDSDG 3 of ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all people at all ages. Our platform therefore provides opportunities for growth and development for all actors involved, helping to sustainably create a positive impact for generations to come.
